About Me

Hello there! I'm Michelle, a full stack software engineer. Previously, I was a pediatric healthcare provider. During the pandemic, I was providing telehealth sessions and searching for interactive activities for my young clients. I noticed there was a lack of online activities that were both engaging and developmentally appropriate. My interest in software development grew and I enrolled at Ada Developers Academy where I learned computer science fundamentals, key concepts and frameworks of software development, and gained hands-on experience building full stack web applications. As I continue to embark on my journey into the tech field, I hope to leverage my healthcare skills while being a part of creating solutions.
